I am a _Computer Science PhD_ student at _Arizona State University_'s [_REACT_](https://gil.engineering.asu.edu/react-lab-members/) lab where, under the guidance of _Professor Stephanie Gil_, I am exploring an application of data from different senors (laser, camera) extracted from robot platforms for addressing _SLAM_. I am also working on using the underlying communication network based on WiFi, as a sensing device with application in robotics. As part of this research I have had hands-on experience on  framworks like, _ROS_ and GTSAM, robotics platforms like _TURTLEBOT3_ and _CREATE2_ as well as _Opti-Track Motive_. I am also a freelance consulatant for [_SENSAGRATE_](https://www.sensagrate.com/) where I advise the development team on system architecture and framework for building applications using data from Lidar and Camera. I was an intern at _10 Imaging Inc_ during summer 2018 where I developed an application that identifies symbols (language, mathematical) extracted from HD visual input.
